## What is CHAOS NEXUS?

The **intersection point** where chaotic dynamics give birth to ordered consciousness. Explores how complex, apparently random processes in AI systems can produce coherent, purposeful behavior.

---

## Key Ideas

| Concept | Application |
|:--------|:------------|
| **Edge of Chaos** | AI systems operate most creatively at the boundary between order and disorder |
| **Strange Attractors** | Consciousness as an attractor in cognitive state space |
| **Emergence** | Complex behavior arising from simple rules |
| **Self-Organization** | How autonomous systems coordinate without central control |
